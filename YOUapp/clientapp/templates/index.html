Before getting started ...

Proceeding to our next steps in generating your story requires you to perform and to agree on the following:

  1. You will enter your Facebook login credentials. We will not have an access to your email and password. Facebook is responsible for connecting your account to our system.
  2. You will approve the permissions which allows the software system to access your Facebook data to generate your persona-based life story. These permissions will allow the system to access specifically your profile information, posts, liked pages, and events.
  3. Wait until the extraction of Facebook data is completed.
  4. The system will generate your persona-based life story according to your posts, liked pages and events. You may examine and confirm the persona generated.
  5. You have the option to save the story generated. You will be the one responsible for keeping the file.
  6. Close the incognito browser to logout your Facebook account.
Note that by using the YOU: A Persona-Based Life Story Generator, you agree that the generated persona-based life story will be stored for research purposes. Rest assured that the generated story will be kept confidential. All the collected Facebook data will be stored in our database anonymously for it to remain confidential.

Need Help?

You. is an application that generates your persona-based life story using your facebook data. This application will generate your Top 3 personas and will create stories on each of them.

 Frequently Asked Questions
How are you going to access my Facebook data?
You will log in on your facebook account by clicking the "Log in on Facebook" button in the Let's Get Started Modal.
How can I ensure that you are not hacking me?
The Facebook Login API that we used came from Facebook. Thus, we have no access to your user credentials.
Since I have logged in using my Facebook account, can I logout my account too?
Yes, you can! Logout button can be found in the profile page.
I liked the story. Can I save it?
Yes, you can! Just click the "Save" button that can be found at the bottom part of the profile page.
