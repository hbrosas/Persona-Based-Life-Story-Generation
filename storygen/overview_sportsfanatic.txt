Jorge Francisco is 21 years old. He is born on April 07 1996. He currently lives in Manila, Philippines. His hometown is Parañaque. He studies at De La Salle University-Manila, studied at Colegio San Agustin – Makati last 2014 and studies at De La Salle University. He is working as Defender at Manila Tala F.C. since December 2015, worked as College President at University Student Government, DLSU since December 2015 until July 2017 and worked as Legislative Assembly Representative at University Student Government, DLSU since January 2014 until August 2016.
